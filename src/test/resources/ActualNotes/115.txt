IT WAS REPORTED IN THE JOURNAL ARTICLE TITLED, "UNPROTECTED LEFT MAIN STENTING IN THE REAL WORLD: TWO-YEAR OUTCOMES OF THE FRENCH LEFT MAIN TAXUS REGISTRY" THAT POST CORONARY DRUG ELUTING STENTING TREATMENT PROCEDURE, STENT THROMBOSIS AND PT DEATH OCCURRED. THE LESION, LOCATED IN THE UNPROTECTED LEFT MAIN (LM) ARTERY, TREATED IN THE INDEX PROCEDURE, WAS STENTED WITH AN UNSPECIFIED TAXUS EXPRESS2 DRUG ELUTING STENT. APPROX SIXTEEN MONTHS POST PROCEDURE, VERY LATE STENT THROMBOSIS AND PT DEATH OCCURRED. NO FURTHER INFO IS AVAILABLE.
