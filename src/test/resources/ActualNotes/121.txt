IT WAS REPORTED IN THE JOURNAL ARTICLE TITLED, "UNPROTECTED LEFT MAIN STENTING IN THE REAL WORLD: TWO-YEAR OUTCOMES OF THE FRENCH LEFT MAIN TAXUS REGISTRY" THAT POST CORONARY DRUG ELUTING STENTING TREATMENT PROCEDURE, STENT THROMBOSIS AND PT DEATH OCCURRED. THE 70% STENOSED LESION, LOCATED IN THE UNPROTECTED DISTAL LEFT MAIN (LM) ARTERY, WAS STENTED WITH AN UNSPECIFIED TAXUS EXPRESS2 DRUG ELUTING STENT. TWENTY MONTHS POST PROCEDURE, VERY LATE STENT THROMBOSIS AND PT DEATH OCCURRED. NO FURTHER INFO IS AVAILABLE.
