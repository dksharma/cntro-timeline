AS IT WAS REPORTED BY THE LEGAL DEPARTMENT VIA EMAIL, A PT HAD A CYPHER STENT IMPLANTED IN THE RIGHT CORONARY ARTERY IN 2003, AFTER HAVING AN ABNORMAL THALLIUM STRESS TEST WHICH SHOWED INFERIOR WALL ISCHEMIA. POST STENTING, THE PT WAS PRESCRIBED PLAVIX FOR 3 MONTHS. APPROX FIVE MONTHS POST INDEX PROCEDURE, THE PT SUFFERED THROMBOSIS AT THE SITE OF THE STENT, WHICH LED TO MYOCARDIAL INFARCTION AND DEATH SIX MONTHS LATER.
