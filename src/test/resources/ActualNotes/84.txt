THE PT HAD A VERY LATE THROMBOSIS OF THREE IMPLANTED CYPHER STENTS. HE WAS ADMITTED WITH CHEST PAIN AND WAS PRE-SHOCK CONDITION. FOUR DAYS AFTER PERCUTANEOUS CORONARY INTERVENTION TO TREAT THE THROMBOSIS, THE PT DIED. IT WAS REPORTED THAT THE DEATH WAS NOT DUE TO THE CORONARY ARTERY INTERVENTION BUT WAS DUE TO HEMORRHAGIC SHOCK POSSIBLY FROM RETROPERITONEAL HEMATOMA OR HEMORRHAGE FROM DIGESTIVE TRACT.
